# Intelligent Brief — Marketing Video Script
## Format: 90-second hero video (web + LinkedIn)

---

### VISUAL DIRECTION
Dark, cinematic. Deep blue-black backgrounds. Clean typography animated on screen.
No stock footage of "people in meetings" — this is data, decisions, clarity.
Music: Tense, minimal piano or ambient electronic (think Apple / Linear).

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[0:00–0:08] COLD OPEN — The noise**

*Screen: Black. Text fades in, one line at a time.*

> 200 emails.
> 14 newsletters.
> 3 analyst reports.
> And a Bloomberg alert that just went off.

*Cut to black.*

> It's 6:58am Monday.
> You have a board meeting in 90 minutes.

---

**[0:08–0:18] THE PROBLEM**

*Screen: A blurred flood of news headlines scrolling fast — unintelligible.*

VOICEOVER:
*"The problem isn't access to information. It's that none of it is written for you."*

*Headlines blur. One sharp line cuts through:*

> "OpenAI cuts enterprise pricing 40%."

VOICEOVER:
*"Does that affect your budget? Your CTO's roadmap? Your competitor's advantage?
Nobody tells you."*

---

**[0:18–0:35] THE PRODUCT REVEAL**

*Screen: A clean dark interface appears. The Intelligent Brief logo.*

VOICEOVER:
*"Intelligent Brief is your weekly strategic intelligence report.
Built for your company. Filtered for your role. Ready every Monday at 7am."*

*Screen: Brief sections animate in — smooth, confident.*

> CEO: Strategy & Risk
> CFO: Markets & Capital
> CMO: Competitor Intelligence
> CTO: Tech Disruption

VOICEOVER:
*"We scan 12 live data sources — SEC filings, market data, patent applications, global news —
and Claude AI synthesises it into one clear, opinionated brief."*

---

**[0:35–0:55] THE BRIEF IN ACTION**

*Screen: Animated brief scrolling — real sections, real layout.*

VOICEOVER:
*"Not headlines. Analysis. Every section ends with one thing most briefings never give you:"*

*Screen: A callout box highlights:*

> "What this means for you →"

VOICEOVER:
*"A clear signal. A decision to make. An action to consider.*

*Your competitor just filed three patents in your core product category.
Your primary manufacturing region is facing a new export restriction.
The Fed just shifted its rate path — here's what it means for your capex plan."*

---

**[0:55–1:10] SOCIAL PROOF / CREDIBILITY**

*Screen: Clean data icons — no fake testimonials.*

VOICEOVER:
*"Powered by the same AI that advises Fortune 500 boards.
Built on data from the Federal Reserve, SEC, Bloomberg, and the world's top research institutions."*

*Screen: Source logos animate in.*

> SEC EDGAR · Alpha Vantage · Google News · USPTO · Semantic Scholar · S&P Global

---

**[1:10–1:25] PRICING + CTA**

*Screen: Clean pricing cards — Solo and Team.*

VOICEOVER:
*"Solo from $15 a month. Team of five from $50.*

*Less than one hour of a consultant's time.
Worth more than a week of theirs."*

*Screen: Final frame — logo, tagline, URL.*

> **Intelligent Brief**
> Be the best-informed person in every meeting.
> companybrief.net

VOICEOVER:
*"Request access today."*

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Style references:**
- Linear.app launch video (dark, fast, clean)
- Notion "The all-in-one workspace" ad
- Apple Intelligence campaign

**Shot list (if using screen recording + motion graphics):**
1. Blurred news flood (0:00–0:08) — looping news RSS feed, fast scroll, defocused
2. Headline isolation (0:08–0:18) — single headline snapping sharp
3. Product interface (0:18–0:55) — actual screen recording of the brief, section reveals
4. Source logos (0:55–1:10) — animated grid of data source marks
5. Pricing cards (1:10–1:25) — brief animation from get-started page
6. Final title card (1:20–1:30) — logo, tagline, URL, black background

**AI video tools to use:**
- HeyGen — for AI presenter voiceover
- Runway Gen-3 — for abstract background visuals
- CapCut / DaVinci Resolve — for assembly and titles
- ElevenLabs — for professional voiceover if no presenter
